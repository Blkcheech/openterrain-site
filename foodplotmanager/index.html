Features

Everything you need
for better plots.

Built by a hunter who got tired of notebooks, spreadsheets, and guessing at seeding rates.

📍

Walk the Edge GPS

Walk your plot's perimeter and the app records a GPS boundary. Accuracy indicators, noise filtering, and automatic acreage calculation. Or tap corners on a satellite map.

🧪

Soil Sample Tracking

Log pH, macro and micronutrients, organic matter, CEC, soil temperature, and lab source. Track changes over time for every plot.

🌱

Planting Logs

Record every planting with seed type, rate, method, and date. 22 built-in seed types with university-sourced rates that auto-fill when selected.

🧮

Rate Calculators

Four modes: total seed needed, bags to buy, area covered by what you have, and a lime calculator that pulls pH from your soil samples. Select a plot and acreage auto-fills.

📋

Input Tracking

Log every lime, fertilizer, herbicide, and insecticide application with rate, quantity, cost, and date. Auto-calculates totals from rate × acreage.

📵

Offline First

Everything works without cell service. GPS tracking, data entry, calculations — all local. Your food plot data lives on your device.
